Community Efforts




"Without a sense of caring, there can be no sense of community"

~ Anthony J. De'Angelo





As an advocate for alternative college options, I volunteer my time to bring those resources and information to my community. In 2020 my effort included co-coordinating an event that brought a wide range of educational options outside of college. Giving attendees the ability to sign up and begin training towards a career in a dominating industry that paid a livable wage in the Charlotte area. Amongst these resources were organizations like Road to Hire, YearUp, Central Piedmont Community College (Certificate Program), Charlotte Area Fund, and others. With over 300 attendees, we were not only able to open up the community to the common topic of not going to college but allow our community members the opportunity to grow financially without assuming a great amount of debt. This cause will continue to be important to me and I plan to bring this into any and everything that I do. Professionally and interpersonally.
